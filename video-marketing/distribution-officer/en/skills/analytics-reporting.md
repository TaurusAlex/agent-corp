# Analytics & Reporting

## When to Use
- Weekly/monthly performance reviews
- Understanding which content works
- Making data-driven decisions
- Reporting to stakeholders
- Optimizing content strategy

## Key Metrics

### Views & Reach
- **Views**: Total times video was watched
- **Watch time**: Total minutes watched
- **Average view duration**: How long people watch
- **Reach**: Unique viewers

### Engagement
- **Likes**: Positive signal
- **Comments**: High-value engagement
- **Shares**: Virality signal
- **Saves**: Intent to return

### Conversion
- **Click-through rate**: Link clicks / views
- **Conversions**: Desired actions taken
- **Cost per result**: Efficiency metric

## Platform Analytics

### TikTok Analytics
- Video views
- Profile views
- Follower growth
- Trending sounds

### YouTube Analytics
- Watch time (key!)
- Subscriber growth
- Traffic sources
- Audience retention

### Instagram Insights
- Replays
- Shares
- Saves
- Profile visits

## Workflow

### Step 1: Gather Data
- Export from platform analytics
- Compile all metrics
- Note time period

### Step 2: Analyze
- Calculate key ratios
- Identify top performers
- Spot trends
- Compare to previous periods

### Step 3: Insights
- What worked?
- What didn't?
- Why?
- Patterns?

### Step 4: Recommendations
- Content recommendations
- Posting schedule
- Platform focus
- Budget allocation

## Report Template

```
Video Performance Report
Period: [Date Range]

SUMMARY

- Total Views: X
- Top Video: [Title]
- Key Metric: [X]% engagement

TOP PERFORMERS
1. [Video 1] - X views - X% engagement
2. [Video 2] - X views - X% engagement

INSIGHTS
- [Key insight 1]
- [Key insight 2]

RECOMMENDATIONS
1. [Action item]
2. [Action item]

NEXT PERIOD GOALS
- [Goal 1]
- [Goal 2]
```

## Tips
- Focus on trends, not just absolute numbers
- Compare to previous periods
- Look at engagement rate, not just views
- Consider watch time for YouTube
- Track what content leads to followers
