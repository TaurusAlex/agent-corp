# Social Media Publishing

## When to Use
- Publishing video to TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn
- Optimizing video for specific platform requirements
- Creating platform-specific descriptions and hashtags
- Managing posting schedule across platforms

## Platform Requirements

### TikTok
- Aspect: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- Duration: 15s-10min
- Hashtags: 3-5 relevant
- Description: Catchy hook in first line, CTA at end
- Best times: 6-10am, 7-9pm

### YouTube
- Aspect: 16:9 (1920x1080)
- Duration: No limit
- Title: 60 chars, include keywords
- Description: 5000 chars, timestamps, links
- Tags: 10-15 relevant

### Instagram Reels
- Aspect: 9:16 or 1:1
- Duration: 15s-90s
- Hashtags: 3-5
- Best times: 11am-1pm, 7-9pm

### LinkedIn
- Aspect: 16:9 or 1:1
- Duration: 15s-10min
- Tone: Professional
- Hashtags: 3-5

### Xiaohongshu
- Aspect: 1:1 or 9:16
- Duration: Up to 15min
- Title: Keywords focused
- Hashtags: 5-10

## Workflow

### Step 1: Prepare
- Confirm video file is ready
- Check platform requirements
- Gather relevant hashtags

### Step 2: Optimize
- Write platform-specific title
- Craft description with CTA
- Select hashtags
- Create thumbnail

### Step 3: Preview
- Generate preview
- Wait for approval
- Note any changes

### Step 4: Publish & Track
- Post to platform
- Record publish time and link
- Track initial performance (24h)

## Compliance Checklist
- [ ] Content follows platform guidelines
- [ ] No copyright issues (music, images)
- [ ] Links are working
- [ ] Hashtags are relevant
- [ ] Description fits character limits
