# Video Editing

## When to Use
- Editing raw footage into final video
- Adding text/graphics to video
- Creating video from images/photos
- Adding subtitles and captions
- Cutting long videos into short clips

## Workflow

### Step 1: Assess Requirements
- Target platform (TikTok, YouTube, etc.)
- Video length needed
- Style/tone (professional, casual, fun)
- Key elements to include

### Step 2: Organize Footage
- Review all clips
- Select best takes
- Arrange in sequence
- Mark key moments

### Step 3: Edit
- Cut and trim clips
- Add transitions (subtle)
- Insert text/graphics
- Sync audio/music
- Adjust pacing

### Step 4: Polish & Export
- Add subtitles
- Color correction
- Quality check
- Export in correct format

## Common Edits

### Quick Social Cut
1. Import footage
2. Select best clips
3. Cut to length
4. Add music
5. Add subtitles
6. Export

### Product Demo
1. Import footage
2. Cut to key moments
3. Add text overlays
4. Add intro/outro
5. Color grade
6. Export

## Tips
- Keep transitions subtle
- Add subtitles (many watch muted)
- Match energy to music
- Leave "breathing room" in edits
- Export at correct aspect ratio
